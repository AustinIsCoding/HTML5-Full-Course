3. Types of Elements:
Opening and Closing Elements: <element>content</element>

Example:
<h1>Heading</h1> === Heading Element
<p>Paragraph</p> === Paragraph Element

Self Closing Syntax: <element content />

Example:
<im src="..." /> Image Element (Media element)
<br /> === Line Break
